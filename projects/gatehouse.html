Modelling the Abbey Gatehouse, Bristol

Maya

Introduction

Bristol has an eclectic combination of architectural styles, ranging from the medieval to 20th century brutalism and beyond. Buildings from most of the architectural periods of the United Kingdom can be seen throughout the city.

Reconstruction of a building from the streets of the city, aiming to recreate the complex geometry only - no texture or shading. Precise 3D representation. The Great Gatehouse (also known as Abbey Gatehouse) is located on College Green, next to the Bristol Central Library.

Planning

Modelling accurately requires powerful observation skills, careful study of real life objects and attention to detail and irregularities. This requires the creation of orthographic sketches, reference images and photographs.
